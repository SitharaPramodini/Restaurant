Mutton Rolls Bliss

Start your meal with Mutton Rolls, crispy pastry rolls filled with seasoned minced mutton, making for a savory and satisfying bite.

Fish Cutlets

French Fries

Indulge in our golden and crispy French Fries, a timeless classic that guarantees the perfect crunch with every bite.

Burger

Treat yourself to our mouthwatering Burger, a culinary delight that combines a juicy, crisp vegetables toasted bun.

Rice and Curry


Experience the rich flavors of Sri Lanka with our signature Rice and Curry. A medley of aromatic spices complements tender meats and vibrant vegetables.

Indulge in the spicy goodness of Devilled Chicken, a tantalizing dish where succulent chicken pieces are stir-fried with peppers, onions, and a zesty sauce.
